Our Services

We offer flexible options to fit landowners and professionals at all levels — from a simple walkthrough to turnkey property transformation.

Tier 1: Property Walkthrough & Consultation

A detailed site walk to evaluate habitat potential, timber value, invasive threats, and access or layout weaknesses. We'll provide recommendations in plain terms, tailored to your goals.

Tier 2: Habitat Plan + Timber & Wildlife Strategy

A written plan and map overlays detailing phased improvements, stand selection, planting suggestions, bedding strategies, travel corridor development, and soil/water recommendations. Great for self-directed landowners or contractors needing clear next steps.

Tier 3: Full-Service Implementation

We coordinate, execute, and monitor the entire land plan — handling subcontractors, forestry oversight, permits, planting schedules, and ongoing stewardship. Our crew can handle the boots-on-ground execution for you.
