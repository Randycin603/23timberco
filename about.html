About 23 Timber Co.

23 Timber Co. was founded on a simple idea: land deserves more than surface-level care. We don’t plant gimmick plots or bulldoze for photos. We walk the land, listen, and plan for what it needs — and what you want it to become.

Whether you're restoring neglected woods, improving wildlife access, or planning a generational property, we approach every project with the same principle:

Stewardship over shortcuts.

Based in New England, we specialize in habitat design, timber stand improvement, and long-term land use planning. We’re not here for quick kills or cookie-cutter layouts — we’re here to help your land thrive.
