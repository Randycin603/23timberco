The Problem

Most landowners don’t have a land problem — they have a strategy problem. Trails in the wrong spots. Timber thinned without purpose. Water ignored. Bedding left to chance. We founded 23 Timber Co. to fix that.

Our Approach

Our plans are built from practical field experience and ecological best practices — aligned with the principles of NDA, QDM, and U.S. Forest Service timber guidance. We aren’t affiliated — we’re informed.

Where We're Headed

We scale from one-acre kill plots to full property transformations. Whether you’re a landowner restoring habitat or a contractor prepping land for long-term yield, we bring strategy, structure, and systems thinking.
