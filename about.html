About 23 Timber Co.

23 Timber Co. was built to address a growing problem in land management: fragmented thinking. Too often, landowners are guided to focus on one element — food plots, timber harvests, access roads — without regard for how it fits into the broader system of wildlife movement, seasonal needs, and long-term habitat health.

We take a holistic approach grounded in experience and ecological principles. Every property is different, but the fundamentals remain: strategic bedding, diverse native food sources, controlled access, secure water, and timber that supports both wildlife and future value.

This company was founded by someone who has spent years managing complex construction projects at the executive level while applying the same systems-based thinking to hunting land and forest planning. That blend of logistical oversight and field experience shapes every project we take on.

From one-acre improvements to large-scale, multi-phase land strategies, we’re built to scale. Whether working directly with landowners or supporting contractors in the field, we bring a structured, outcomes-driven approach to every job — grounded in what works and backed by science. If you’re looking for more than a patchwork plan, 23 Timber Co. is ready to walk your land.
