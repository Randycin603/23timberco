Frequently Asked Questions

What areas do you serve?

Primarily New Hampshire and Eastern Massachusetts, but we’re open to travel for the right projects.

Do you only work on hunting properties?

Nope. We specialize in wildlife and forest-based properties, but we also assist landowners focused on timber, recreation, access, or resale value.

Can I hire you just for a walkthrough or plan?

Yes — advisory-only services are available. We also offer phased work if you're not ready to execute everything at once.

Do you do all the work yourself?

Not always. We self-perform when it makes sense, but we also partner with trusted contractors and equipment operators when scale demands it.

What does a typical job cost?

It depends on scope. Projects range from $500 consults to $50,000+ multi-phase land transformations. We’ll always define the scope before work starts.

How soon can you start?

We try to book site walks within 2 weeks and field work based on seasonal access, permitting, and urgency. We'll be upfront about timing from day one.
