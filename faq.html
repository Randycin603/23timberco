Frequently Asked Questions

What areas do you serve?

Primarily New Hampshire and Eastern Massachusetts, but we’re open to travel for the right projects.

Do you only work on hunting properties?

No. We specialize in wildlife and forest-based properties, but also assist landowners focused on timber, recreation, access, or resale value.

Can I hire you just for a walkthrough or plan?

Yes — advisory-only services are available. We also offer phased work.

Do you do all the work yourself?

We self-perform when it makes sense, and partner with trusted contractors when scale demands it.

What does a typical job cost?

From $500 consults to $50,000+ multi-phase transformations. Scope is defined up front.

How soon can you start?

Site walks within ~2 weeks; field work depends on seasonal access, permitting, and urgency.
